◆ Program mechanics

Three tiers · rolling stipend.

Every ambassador delivers a defined post cadence per week + accepts a defined number of brand-affiliate slots per month. Stipend is monthly. Amplification from Pylon's channels is automatic on qualifying posts.

◆ Apply

Rolling review · most decisions in 7 days.

Applications are reviewed weekly. Tier assignment happens at acceptance based on your handles, audience, and content history. Not every applicant is accepted — the program is capped at 100 active ambassadors across all tiers.
