What the record actually is

Highlight reels don’t compound. The record does.

A highlight reel gets a scout to hit play. A record gets a scout to make the call. The gap between the two is film discipline, coach outreach that lands, an evaluation with structure, and a Player Transcript recruiters can actually verify. That’s the standard.

On TOJ Sports, every signed athlete works under the same evaluation framework — Prospect Edge grading, the CollegeFootballRecruiting.com roster, the College Directory across seven division tiers. Trainers running their own operating system inherit all of it on day one.

The upside isn’t just offers. It’s offers you can trust — because the process behind them is documented, verified, and repeatable. When the next class comes up, the standard doesn’t reset. It compounds.

The value ladder

Eight rungs. From lead gen to authority.

Every service TOJ Sports delivers stacks on the one below it. Lead generation is the foundation. Authority is the peak. Each rung is a job the standard actually performs — not a marketing promise.

01

Lead Generation

Bringing new athletes, trainers, sponsors, media partners, and program contacts into the pipeline through intake forms, tracked outreach, and content that earns replies.

02

Sales

Converting interest into signed rosters, booked evaluations, paid media engagements, and trainer blueprint purchases. Structured pitch, structured close, structured onboarding.

03

Recruiting Opportunities / Athlete Development

Placing athletes into camps, college looks, pro exposures, and structured development plans. Not a highlight reel — a career pipeline.

04

Recruiting Media

The film, the edits, the Subject Report evaluations — the media that actually carries recruiting weight because it's produced against a verified standard.

05

Visibility

Getting real audiences — coaches, recruiters, sponsors, peers — to see the athlete, the trainer, the results. Not just reach; the right eyes.

06

Community

The peer roster: athletes, trainers, and creators who lift each other. The Athletes Certified ecosystem is not a list — it's a network that generates its own opportunities.

07

Brand

Positioning each participant with a durable identity — not just a nickname or a highlight reel. What people say when the athlete or trainer isn't in the room.

08

Authority

The apex rung. Becoming the room others check before deciding — the trainer other trainers cite, the athlete other athletes name-drop, the report scouts trust by default.

Every trainer and program on TOJ Sports has all eight rungs running underneath — even when the athlete only signed up for one. That’s how the record compounds instead of resetting each season.

The infrastructure underneath

Two platforms, one credential trail.

Every athlete accepted onto the roster gets a Prospect Edge profile — an evaluation with structure, film, verified physicals, academic record, and an offers ledger. This is what recruiters actually check.

Alongside the profile, every client gets the College Directory — the working list of coaches across every level of college football, from FBS through NAIA, with academic requirements and a live database that flips the recruiting flow: colleges can find the athlete, not the other way around.

Prospect Edge · What's on the profile
  • Verified physicals — height, weight, 40 time, squat, bench
  • Academic record — GPA, ACT, SAT, transcript uploads
  • Film library — workout video, highlight link, on-field transcript
  • Offers ledger — every offer tracked, with the commit path
  • Player links — social handles rolled into one credential trail
College Directory · What every client gets
  • Coach contacts — NAIA · D3 · D2 · D1 · FCS · FBS
  • Academic requirements — per-school minimums, application deadlines
  • Live athlete database — colleges browse the roster, not the other way around
  • Cross-referenced — athletic + academic fit filters
