The flow

Relationships → Opportunities → Revenue → Media.

The eight things below the line — foundation, framework, practice, documentation, visibility, community, brand, authority — are the growth path. The four things at the center are the engine.

Every athlete's NIL journey starts with a real relationship — mentor, coach, parent, teacher. Every relationship, when the standard is right, becomes an opportunity — a free class booking, a cohort seat, a Player Transcript, a first legit NIL deal. Opportunities produce revenue. Revenue funds better media and better mentorship. And better media multiplies the next round of relationships.

TOJ Advisory teaches the full circle — SubjectSkillz educates, TOJ tracks and connects, SubjectMedia produces. You keep the upside.

The value ladder

Eight rungs. From lead gen to authority.

Every service TOJ Advisory delivers stacks on the one below it. Foundation is at the bottom. NIL representation-readiness is the peak. Each rung is a job the SubjectSkillz curriculum actually performs — not a marketing promise.

01

Foundation

Identity, position, and NIL literacy at a starter level. Who you are as an athlete and creator, and what "valid business purpose" actually means under 2026 rules.

02

Framework

NIL rules, contract literacy, brand system, compliance guardrails — the working structure that separates the athletes who get paid from the athletes who get burned.

03

Practice Reps

Live sessions, mentor calls, mock brand deals, contract walkthroughs, social audits. The actual work of getting ready — logged on the SubjectReport LMS.

04

Documentation

Player Transcript, Prospect Edge record, coursework certificate, financial-literacy attestation. The proof-of-work trail recruiters and sponsors can actually verify.

05

Visibility

SubjectMedia-produced content, promo-code campaigns, dashboard-tracked exposure. Getting real audiences — coaches, recruiters, sponsors, peers — to see verified work, not curated hype.

06

Community

Curated cohort — athletes moving through the 6-week program together, referring, comparing notes, backing each other. The room that becomes a network for the rest of your career.

07

Brand

A distinct identity — voice, story, position, financial hygiene — coached by the three brand voices behind SubjectSkillz. What people say when the athlete isn’t in the room.

08

Authority

Ready for real NIL representation. The room that scouts, agents, and brands check before deciding — because the transcript, the coursework, and the financial hygiene are already on record.

Every athlete on TOJ Advisory has all eight rungs running underneath — even when the family only enrolls for one module.

The funnel

Four stages. From free class to full representation.

The advisory funnel is deliberate. Start free. Earn the cohort seat. Graduate with a Business Diagnostic. Step into representation when the work is on record — not before. Every stage runs against the same standard behind SubjectSkillz, TOJ, and SubjectMedia.

Top of funnel · Free tier

NIL Wealth Starter Class

The free entry point — a self-paced starter class covering NIL basics, red flags, and what compliant deals actually look like. Delivered through the SubjectReport LMS. No cost, no commitment — the door into the system.

Delivers — Foundation · Framework
Mid funnel · Cohort program

The 6-Week Cohort

The core paid program. Six weeks, three modules, live cohort — NIL foundations & compliance, personal branding, financial literacy. Runs on the SubjectReport LMS with the Subject Course Platform tracking assessments, certificates, and cohort progress.

Delivers — Practice Reps · Documentation · Community
Bottom of funnel · Diagnostic

NIL Business Diagnostic

The graduation deliverable — a personalized report covering Personal Brand Income Potential, Risk Exposure (taxes, contracts, brand misalignment, reputation), and a business diagnostic that turns the cohort work into a plan you can execute.

Delivers — Brand · Authority
Full circle · Representation pathway

NIL Representation Pathway

The commercial arm — where trained, compliant, documented athletes step into real representation. Deal brokerage, brand partnerships, piggyback deals, and the full-circle wealth-pipeline monetization. Optional. Earned.

Delivers — Authority · Revenue
Who TOJ Advisory serves

Four archetypes. One curriculum.

If you’re a student-athlete, a parent, a school program, or a young sports creator, TOJ Advisory is the front door. What differs is which stage of the funnel you enter through — not the standard, and not the curriculum.

Archetype 01

Student-Athletes

High school and college athletes navigating NIL — before the mistakes get made. NIL fundamentals, contracts, taxes, and the film discipline that turns highlights into recruiting weight.

Archetype 02

Parents & Guardians

The people making the call alongside the athlete. Donation-eligible support of SubjectSkillz, tax-deductible under the 501(c)(3) structure, plus a clear seat at the education table.

Archetype 03

Coaches & School Programs

Athletic directors, high school programs, 7v7 clubs deploying the 6-week NIL Readiness Certification as a schoolwide or teamwide offering — with annual contracts and per-athlete pricing.

Archetype 04

Young Sports Creators

Camera operators, editors, on-field storytellers on the SubjectSkillz-to-SubjectMedia path — where curriculum becomes paid production work with real deliverables.

Tier 02 · in practice

Every cohort ships against one curriculum — with room for every athlete’s arc.

When a school, program, or 7v7 organization deploys the 6-week NIL Readiness Certification, every athlete on the roster gets the same curriculum, the same assessments, the same certificate at the end. Peer cohort effects compound underneath.
