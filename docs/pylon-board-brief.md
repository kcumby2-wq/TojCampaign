## Why this partnership · in one paragraph

Pylon has mature infrastructure. What Pylon does not have is a scaled-delivery layer for the 1,261 teams and 117,904 athletes touched by its events. TOJ delivers that layer through 20 productized revenue lines, a national Regional Coordinator crew, a proprietary Momentum Score dataset that becomes an insurmountable data moat, and portable operating IP that Pylon licenses for the term. **The alternative — Pylon corp building this internally — would require ~$3M+ of capex, 18–24 months, and material distraction from the core rankings + event operations business.** TOJ delivers all of it in 6 weeks of build time on the branch that has already begun (`claude/pylon-partnership-2026` in the tojcampaign repo).

## The 3 operational domains TOJ owns

**Domain 1 · Scouting.** Digital Scouts on-field grading crews at every Pylon event. Subject Report athlete profiles at scale. Integration with Optimum grading. Feeder to Sports Thread's ranking pipeline. Recruiting Concierge outreach agent. HBCU pipeline.

**Domain 2 · Creative.** Subject Media production of every Team Media Package. Live Event Console for real-time content during games. Per-athlete hype content. Sponsor-integrated content. 4 content pillars (Ritual · Scale · Prestige · Capture) authored on TOJ infrastructure under Pylon voice.

**Domain 3 · Efficiency.** TOJ automation of every sales sequence pre/during/post event. CRM nurture. Package upsell flows. Sponsor fulfillment reporting. Merch drop ops. City League Host Toolkit + Ownership Fund. 5 Regional Coordinators for national delivery parity + 1 international contract.

**Kyron's TOJ scope covers 4 functions across these 3 domains:** Tech, Sales, Scouting, and Media. He is the accountable operator; the Pylon-hired Creative Lead runs day-to-day content on the TOJ system.
