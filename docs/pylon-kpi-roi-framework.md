## Section 7 · Reporting cadence and format

### Monthly to Pylon owner (5th business day of following month)

- One-page dashboard (Section 1 + Section 4 rollup)
- Top 3 wins · top 3 misses · top 3 for next month
- Cash position + AR aging
- Any owner-time-saved metric changes
- Any escalation items requiring owner attention

### Quarterly to Board of Advisors + Directors (within 15 days of quarter close)

- Full KPI dashboard (all 7 sections)
- Year-1 target vs. YTD actual · projected full-year
- Trend commentary
- Risk register update
- Forward outlook + strategic decisions requiring board input

### Annual to Board (within 30 days of fiscal year close)

- Full-year vs. target performance
- Year-2 forecast + investment ask (if any)
- Strategic optionality update (2nd sport partnership · B2B Data Feed licensees · Alumni Fund AUM)
- Renewal decision on TOJ partnership (auto-renew unless 60-day notice)
