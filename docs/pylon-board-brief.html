◆ 02 · The Frame

What TOJ does. What TOJ does not touch.

Pylon has mature infrastructure. Sports Thread runs rankings. Team Inn runs housing. Top-tier sponsors are booked with multi-year renewals + category exclusivity. Registration, scheduling, officiating are built in. TOJ builds nothing parallel.

What stays untouched

  • ▸ Sports Thread — rankings authorship stays with Sports Thread. TOJ feeds grade + stat data that enriches accuracy.
  • ▸ Team Inn — every TOJ-produced event page, team package, and family communication surfaces the Team Inn booking flow.
  • ▸ Jordan Brand / Wilson / Muscle Milk / Battle / Overtime / BallerTV / Whistle — all top-tier sponsors stay Pylon's.
  • ▸ Registration + scheduling + officiating — Pylon corp's operational infrastructure stays as-is.

◆ 11 · Risk & Mitigation

What could go wrong.

Risk · Optimum sub-agreement stalls

Blocks SKUs #1c, #2, #3, #9-Combine, #11, #20 launch. Direct revenue impact ~$500K-$1M in Year 1.

Mitigation

Kyron leads the conversation with named Optimum contact (BD lead or founder). Weekly status reported to Pylon owner. If unresolved by Q2 2026, TOJ ships ungraded versions of dependent SKUs at reduced pricing while renegotiating.

Risk · Regional Coordinator hiring delays

Understaffed territory can't hit delivery parity. Coverage risk in Southeast/Midwest.

Mitigation

Hire sequence follows Kyron's physical presence — earliest hires get direct onboarding. Subject Media contract crew backfills coverage during any gaps. Midwest Q4 2026 → Q1 2027 defer option preserves cash flow if needed.

Risk · Category sponsor exclusivity conflict

Mid-market TOJ deal accidentally conflicts with Pylon's Jordan/Wilson/Muscle Milk exclusivity.

Mitigation

Every sponsor deal checks `pylon_sponsors.category` against signed exclusivity. Server-side enforcement in the CRM. All deals >$10K require Kyron approval; Kyron approval requires Pylon-corp exclusivity check.

Risk · AI Coach quality perception

Coach community rejects AI-authored guidance as "wrapper on ChatGPT."

Mitigation

AI Coach is trained on real Pylon grade + game data — not generic prompts. Every brief cites source data. First 30 coach cohort onboarded with hand-tuning to establish quality baseline. Quarterly voice + accuracy audit.

Risk · Data Feed sub-agreement extensions

Optimum + Sports Thread must bless the licensed data feed (their data components).

Mitigation

Both sub-agreements extend during initial term negotiation. If either stalls, TOJ ships Data Feed with only its own proprietary data (Momentum Score + Pylon event volume + engagement) at reduced pricing.

Risk · Ambassador program compliance

Minor athletes + NCAA-eligible college athletes = complex 1099 + FTC + NCAA rules.

Mitigation

Program launches in `applied_pending_review` mode until CLO signs off on stipend flow. Compliance audit monthly. Third-party CPA firm for 1099 processing. NCAA compliance officer on retainer.
