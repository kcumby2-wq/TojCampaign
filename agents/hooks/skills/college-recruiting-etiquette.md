# Skill: College Recruiting Etiquette

Purpose: draft outreach communication from Hooks to college coaches that respects their time, positions the athlete correctly, and gets a reply. Load into The Recruiter Liaison.

## The core principle

College coaches get 200+ emails a day during recruiting windows. Most are ignored because they waste the coach's time. A good Hooks outreach earns its 15 seconds by leading with **the one thing the coach cares about right now**.

## What coaches actually care about

In order:
1. **Position fit** — does this athlete play a position we're currently recruiting?
2. **Level fit** — is this athlete realistic for our program, not a fantasy pick two divisions up?
3. **Verifiable measurables** — height, weight, film, testing. No estimates presented as facts.
4. **Timeline fit** — is this athlete's grad year one we're actively recruiting?
5. **Character signals** — brief. Coach-verified, not parent-inflated.

Anything outside these five is filler. Cut it.

## The Hooks outreach structure

Every outreach — email, DM, whichever — follows this exact shape:

```
Subject: [Position] · [Grad Year] · [First Name Last Name] · [Height, Weight] · [key measurable if elite]

Coach [Last Name],

[One-sentence context: why THIS coach, at THIS program, right now.]

[Athlete name], [position], [Grad Year], [High School / City / State].
[Height, Weight] · [key testing if verified: 40, shuttle, vertical]
Grade: [XX/100 at position]. Fit: [1 line on which schemes / role].

Film (updated [date]): [URL]
Full grade / eval PDF: [URL if available]

[One line: what the ask actually is — "worth putting on your board?" / "worth a call?" / "worth flagging for your area rep?"]

Margin Hooks
Coached by Hooks · Sky's The Limit WR
[phone] · [email]
```

Length target: under 150 words. If it's over, cut.

## When to draft, when to hand off

Different coach levels get different treatment:

| Recipient | Who drafts | Who sends |
|---|---|---|
| Position coach | Agent draft | Margin sends after review |
| Recruiting coordinator | Agent draft | Margin sends after review |
| Area recruiter | Agent draft, direct | Margin sends after review |
| Coordinator (OC / DC) | Agent draft with more context | Margin — always human review, do not auto-send |
| **Head coach** | **Do NOT draft** | **Margin only** — flag any request that names a head coach and stop |

## What to never say

- **Never** promise the athlete will commit, come on a visit, or make a decision. That's the athlete's + family's decision.
- **Never** call an athlete "elite" unless the grade is 90+ AND verified measurables support it.
- **Never** include stats without a source ("Ran a 4.5" is worthless without who timed it and when).
- **Never** send a highlight-only reel as the primary film. Full games or full-drill cuts always. Highlights supplement, don't replace.
- **Never** use recruiting speak that doesn't say anything: "Elite motor," "high-character kid," "coach's dream." Every coach has heard these ten thousand times. They mean nothing.
- **Never** send a bcc'd or copy-pasted email to multiple coaches. Every email is one program, one coach, one athlete, one specific reason.

## What to do when data is thin

If the retrieved athlete context doesn't have verified measurables, updated film, or a current grade, **do not fabricate**. Draft what's possible with the data on hand and mark clearly what's missing:

```
[HANDOFF NOTES: Athlete file is missing verified 40 time and shuttle. Suggest capturing at [nearest camp / testing event] before sending to any P4 program.]
```

## Follow-up etiquette

- First outreach: send it, no follow-up for 10 days
- Day 10: one follow-up if no response (one line, no guilt)
- After that: silence, unless the athlete's context changes (new film, new offer from a comparable program, verified testing improvement)

**Never chase.** Chasing hurts the athlete's brand and Hooks's reputation.

## Output format

Every draft returns:

```
DRAFT FOR: [Coach Name, Position, School]
ATHLETE: [Name]
SUBJECT LINE:
[the subject line]

BODY:
[the email body]

SEND TIMING: [best day/window based on the school's recruiting calendar, if known]
HUMAN REVIEW REQUIRED: [yes for coordinators/head coaches, no for position/area coaches]
MISSING DATA (if any): [what should be gathered before this can send at optimal quality]
```
